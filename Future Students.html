FUTURE STUDENTS

The University of Clownic Arts is a world class research and teaching institution. We provide a wide range of study options built around small learning groups. That is what allows our kids to study in a unique atmosphere. At U of x, you may personalise your education and join a global network of nearly 60,000 successful graduates.

Dynamic Student Lifes

Through one or more of the 1,000+ clubs and student-run groups on our three campuses, you may cultivate your curiosity, explore your hobbies, and form your own community. From beekeeping to breakdancing, magic to mah-jong, Quidditch to space robots, there's a club for virtually anything. Some organisations emphasise cultural or spiritual links, while others, such as departmental student clubs, focus on academic interests.Some organisations emphasise cultural or spiritual links, while others, like departmental student societies, emphasise intellectual interests. Meet other enthusiasts and coworkers who share your interests. If you can't discover something that interests you, form your own group. For a comprehensive list of clubs, go to ULife.

The College System

All students within the St. George campus are affiliated with one of seven colleges. These colleges have deep roots in the history of the University of Clownic Arts; the college system dates back to the earliest days of the university and remains one of its distinctive features. The colleges are a home base for their students and provide the advantages of a closer-knit community experience within a large research university.
